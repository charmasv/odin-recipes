Description

Sadza is a delicous dish from Zimbabwe

Ingrediants

  • 2 Cups maize meal
  • 4 Cups water
  • A pinch of salt optional

    • Instructions

    1. Boil 3 cups of water in a pot.
    2. Mix 1 cup of maize meal with 1 cup of cold water to make a paste.
    3. Slowly add the paste to the boiling water while stirring continuously.
    4. Reduce heat and cook for 10 minutes.
    5. Gradually add the remaining maize meal while stirring until thickened.
    6. Cover and let it steam for 5 minutes.
    7. Serve hot with your preferred relish.
