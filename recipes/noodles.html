Ingrediants

  • 200g dried noodles (egg noodles, rice noodles, or instant noodles)

  • 4 cups water

  • 1 tablespoon oil (optional, for preventing sticking)

  • Salt to taste

  • Optional toppings: soy sauce, sesame oil, vegetables, cooked meat, or eggs

Instructions

  1. Bring water to boil in a large pot

  2. Add the noodles and cook according to package instructions (usually 3-5 minutes).

  3. Drain the noodles and rinse under cold water if you want to stop the cooking process.

  4. Toss with a bit of oil to prevent sticking (optional).

  5. Add your favorite sauce, toppings, or broth.
