Ingrediants

  • 1 cup rice (white or brown)

  • 2 cups water

  • 1/2 tablespoon salt (optional)

  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil (optional)

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear.

  2. In a pot, bring water to a boil and add salt (if using).

  3. Add the rice, stir once, and reduce the heat to low.

  4. Cover and let it simmer for 15 minutes (white rice) or 40 minutes (brown rice).

  5. Turn off the heat and let the rice sit for 5 minutes.

  6. Fluff with a fork and serve.
